# Winters surviviors
Make a camp, collect resources and survive as long as you can in the harsh winter!

# Gameplay
- To place a new region:
    - start by **left clicking** on an already exisiting region (**You can only create a region connected to a region before!**).
    - While **holding the button** draw the new region.
    - To end marking the terrain for the region **stop holding the mouse button** while also **being connected to an already existing region**.
- To change the name of a region **right click on top of it**.

## Resources
- Your population will increase by 1 every 5 seconds.
- The resources will be use 1 every second.
To keep your population alive you have to balance between 3 resources (aka 5 values):
- Fuel - You can **mine new fuel** using the fuel extractor building (the first one).
    - **Fuel isn't endless** if it ends in a region that region won't be able to generate new fuel.
- Temperature - The temperature **will automaticly rise** if you have enought fuel 
    - The starting temperature is 15°C (59°F).
    - The tempearture cannot increase above 30°C (86°F).
    - Your **fuel consumption** depends on how big your base is (area of all regions).
    - You will **lose people** if the base temperature reaches below 0°C (32°F).
- Food:
    - You start with 100kg of food
    - **Each person uses 1** kg of food
    - You can **farm new food** by using the farm (second building).
    - You will **lose people** if the food reserves reach 0.
- Water:
    - You start with 100l of water
    - **Each person uses 1**l of water
    - You can **get water** by using the well (third building).
    - **Water isn't endless** if it ends in a region that region won't be able to generate new water.
    - You will **lose people** if the water reserves reach 0.
- Morale:
    - You lose morale every time a person dies.
    - If **morale reaches 0** you lose the game.

## Building
Another important part of the game is the building mechanic. You have 3 structures: the fuel extractor, the farm and the well.
- To place a building:
    - First look in to the **left-bottom side** of your screen.
    - Then **Drag and drop** the icon to where you want to place it.
    - You can only drop it in to an existing region, it will mine the resources of that region.

## ZOOM
- You can zoom in to the canvas by clicking the buttons on the right-bottom side of the screen.
